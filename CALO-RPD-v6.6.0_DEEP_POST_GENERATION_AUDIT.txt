IMPORTANT AUDIT CORRECTION
--------------------------
H19 alleged that `except Exception` catches `KeyboardInterrupt` and `SystemExit`. In Python it does not; both derive from BaseException, not Exception. The independent-validator failure wrapper remains an explicit ordinary-error boundary and records failure. H19 is therefore VERIFIED_NOT_DEFECT, not cosmetically “fixed”.
